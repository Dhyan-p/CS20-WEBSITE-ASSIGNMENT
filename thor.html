Thor

Thor is the Norse God of Thunder and one of Marvel's most powerful superheroes. Based on ancient mythology, he first appeared in Marvel Comics in 1962. Thor comes from Asgard, a realm of gods, and wields the powerful hammer Mjolnir, which grants him control over lightning and storms. His strength, bravery, and nobility make him a natural leader.

Thor's story often focuses on his journey to become worthy. At the beginning, he is arrogant and reckless, but after being sent to Earth to learn humility, he grows into a wiser and more responsible hero. His experiences on Earth help him understand humanity and shape him into a protector of all realms.

Thor has faced countless threats, from mythical creatures to cosmic villains. His biggest battles include fighting Loki, the Frost Giants, Hela, and even Thanos. No matter how strong the enemy, Thor always fights with courage and determination. His godly power and unbreakable spirit make him one of Marvel's strongest heroes.

Beyond his battles, Thor's character also shows emotion and depth. He faces loss, grief, and responsibility as he tries to protect his people. His mix of humor, strength, and heart makes him a beloved character in both comics and movies.
